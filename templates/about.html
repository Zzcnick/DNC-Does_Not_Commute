About

What is DoesNotCommute?

DoesNotCommute is an all in one commuter app made by New Yorkers, for New Yorkers.

Using information from Google Maps and DarkSky, we provide users with reliable, up-to-date information and statistics regarding their commute. We provide it all: from optimal routes, to delays, to alerts, to weather.

Whatever might get in your way from point A to point B, we'll give you the latest. We've got you covered!

You'll never be late again with the help of our fast and efficient app!

How Does It Work?

DoesNotCommute is designed with you in mind! We that when you're on the go, you don't have time for extra, complicated things. That's why we've designed our interface to be user-friendly, with simplicity in mind.

To begin, visit our homepage. Then enter the requested information when prompted, including your desired location, desired time of arrival, and transportation method.

Once you're done, we'll show you the best possible routes, as well as information about the weather and other annoying things that might get in your way on the way there!

If you travel with us, you'll make it there on time, every time. That's our promise!
